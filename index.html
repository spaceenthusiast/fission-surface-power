Fission Surface Power

Advanced nuclear fission technology designed to deliver reliable, long-duration power for lunar bases, habitats, rovers, and future Mars exploration — enabling sustainable human presence beyond Earth.

NASA's Fission Surface Power project (building on Kilopower success) aims to provide at least 40 kilowatts of continuous electricity — enough to power dozens of households — for 10+ years, independent of sunlight or lunar night cycles.
